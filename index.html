Alexey Tregubov - computer scientist
machine learning engineer

Alexey is interested in software architecture and design. His research interests include system and software engineering, quantitative cost and schedule estimation in software projects, data driven process improvement. Alexey Tregubov has three years of industry experience with developing large-scale distributed systems.
Alexey Tregubov is a committed software engineer and scholar, who believes that learning process never stops.

Projects

Ongoing research projects

DATASEM and DES frameworks - Agent based and discrete event simulation frameworks for modeling of various software development processes (Agile, Scrum, Integrated Master Schedule, Kanban-based Scheduling System, etc.). It compares effectiveness (effort, schedule, bottle-necks, governance overhead) of various software development processes and scheduling techniques for different organizational environments.
